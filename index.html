Project 2 · Advanced Engine

Smart Budget — Advanced Edition

SDG 1: No Poverty Alignment

The Problem: Not having stable finances is a big reason why people in Malaysia struggle with poverty. While basic budgeting helps, low-income families and students face severe economic isolation because they are completely cut off from real-world financial literacy resources and changing market dynamics. Furthermore, critical community social safety nets—such as local welfare hubs, NGO food banks, and zakat distribution centers—are highly fragmented and rely entirely on unreliable word-of-mouth. Without a real-time, unified system to discover accessible aid and learn strategic budgeting to counter inflation, vulnerable groups are frequently left unprotected against unexpected financial crises, pushing them deeper below the poverty line.

Our Solution: Smart Budget is an upgraded mobile app, built using Jetpack Compose, that transforms from a basic expense tracker into a comprehensive, tech-driven social safety net to build community resilience. The app empowers users by introducing a decentralized mutual-aid portal alongside its core envelope tracking framework. By allowing users to discover and log real-time relief operations directly on a shared network, the app ensures that financial support structures are transparent and instantly accessible. It directly advances the UN's goal of ending poverty by bridging the information gap, keeping families securely connected to live resource maps and actionable economic insights when they need them most.

Lessons Learned: Managing structural coexistence between background Retrofit threads and live Firebase connection instances requires careful asynchronous scope tracking to maintain smooth application rendering cycles.

Project 1 · Architectural Foundation

Smart Budget — Baseline Framework

The Problem: Not having stable finances is a big reason why people in Malaysia struggle with poverty. A lot of folks, particularly students and those earning less, just don't have good ways to track their spending. This often leads to getting into debt and not being able to put aside money for emergencies. When people don't have a clear system to handle the money they do have, they're much more likely to be hit hard by unexpected financial problems, which can easily push them below the poverty line.

Our Solution: Smart Budget is a mobile app, built using Jetpack Compose, that aims to help people feel more secure about their money. It does this by using a method called 'Envelope Budgeting.' The app gives you a clear, up-to-the-minute look at all your available funds, broken down into important categories. This way, users can easily see what they need to prioritize and ultimately become more financially stable. It directly helps with the UN's goal of ending poverty by empowering people to manage their money better.

Lessons Learned: Implementing a clean unidirectional flow model using StateFlow structures ensures reliable component rendering and prevents interface lag across deeply nested navigation paths.

  • ✓
    5-View Interface Graph — Navigates multi-screen flows without data breakdown errors.
  • ✓

  • ✓
    Envelope Budgeting Engine — Calculates category targets reactively using explicit mathematical rules.
  • ✓

01 · Structural Evolution

From Functional Building Blocks to Production Architecture

Progressing from static layout nodes in Lab 1 to distributed, multithreaded cloud structures in Project 2 radically reshaped my development methodology. Managing Multi-Tier Asynchronous Lifecycles

My primary structural challenge centered on co-ordinating concurrent task loads inside an MVVM pattern.
